About Me

I am a software engineer and game developer from San Jose, California. I currently study at UC Irvine.

If you are interested in seeing what kind of games I have made, or possibly playing them, they are all availible for free on either my Steam Developer page, or my itch.io page.

I also have interesets in mathematics and urban planning, though much less formal experience in the two.

I interned as a software engineer at the Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory from June through September of 2024 where I did programming for satellite networking as well as web traffic generation on a 5g network. I previously interned as a web developer for CodeHS where I helped add new features and tools to the site, fix bugs, and improve accessability for screen-reader users and people with color-blindness.

Outside of professional and academic life, I enjoy cooking, live music, art, and deep dives to random topics on Wikipedia.
