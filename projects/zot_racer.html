Zot Racer

Winter 2023 - 2 months

A racing game developed by a team of 8 students from the UCI Video Game Development Club in the winter of 2023 using the Unreal Engine.

I worked on the project as a gameplay programmer. I programmed all movement, controls, and physics for the car, as well as some supporting features like the logic for lap completion and counting, and sound effects. I also learned how to use Blender to do 3d modeling on this project, and DiVinci Resolve to make the trailer. The game is unofficially one of the top 2 Zot-themed racing games of all time.

There is a free Windows download at the link above.
