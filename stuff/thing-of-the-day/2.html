1/2/26 - Pick Up That Knife

The whole album this song is on is incredible but for some reason this song is just on another level for me.

First, addressing the songs leading up to it, tracks 1 through 3 come in heavy and hard, then Elderberry Wine takes it in a different, but also immaculate direction. Phish Pepsi is fun and kinda funky, then Candy Breath comes in, bringing a bit of a similar vibe before going back to hard and heavy territory. Then we get to Candy Breath which feels like the moment when you finally take a moment to breathe and think over everything and begin to overthink everything as well.

Then, we get to Pick Up That Knife. The opening instrumental is really something. It reminds me somehow both of a building tension, and the feeling of powering through mounting pressure. The instrumentals and lyrics complement each other incredibly well.
